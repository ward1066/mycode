Scope and Limitations

Scope of Initial Release

Release-1 consists of the minimal functionality required to permit users to place orders with the new system. It will not allow buyers the ability to track orders, likewise suppliers/merchants will not be able to report on or manage their sales via ACME Online.

The full contents of relase-1 are summarised in Appendix B: Release 1 scope.

Scope of Subsequent Releases

The remainder of the use-cases and requirements will be delivered in release-2.

Limitations and Exclusions

There are no limitations and exclusions.
