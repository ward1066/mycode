UC6::View Products
Overview
Allows the user to enter some search criteria and browse the resulting list of matching products. Drill-down allows more details to be displayed for items of interest.

Basic Flow
  1. The buyer enters the search criteria. They can enter search terms in free text. Optionally they may also restrict the extent of the search by selecting the product category within which to search.
  2. The buyer can browse the list of products returned.
  3. The buyer can drill-down on a product of interest. Within the drill-down more details / images of the product are available.
  4. The buyer [UC4] adds the item to the shopping cart.
  5. The buyer [UC7] reviews the contents of their shopping cart.
  6. The use-case ends
