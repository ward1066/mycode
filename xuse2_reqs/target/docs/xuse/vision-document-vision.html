Vision of the Solution

Vision Statement

ACME Online will relaunch the ACME Plc. brand and online presence, providing users with a pleasurable and secure online shopping experience that increases their confidence in the ability of ACME Plc. to deliver the right goods every time. Furthermore ACME Online will reduce internal costs, improve supplier management and become the most important sales platform of the company.
